Privacy Policy for Vogadu

Introduction

Vogadu is an AI-powered chat bot designed to answer questions about cars, providing information through Messenger. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of personal information we collect, how we collect it, use it, secure it, and the rights you have in relation to your personal data.

Information Collection and Use

Types of Data Collected:

  • Page-Scoped ID (PSID): We collect your Messenger PSID to uniquely identify your chat session.
  • Chat Logs: We store the conversations between you and Vogadu to improve the bot, fix bugs, and enhance user experience.

Collection Method:

We collect the information directly via the Messenger webhook when you interact with Vogadu.

Use of Data:

We use the collected data solely for the purposes of improving Vogadu, fixing bugs, and enhancing the user experience. We do not use your data for marketing purposes.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share or sell your personal information to third parties.

Data Storage and Security

We store your data in Firebase Firestore, adhering to their security best practices to ensure the protection of your personal information.

Retention of Data

We retain your information for an indefinite period, as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including for the improvement of Vogadu and user experience enhancement.

Your Rights

You have the right to request the deletion of your data.

Exercising Your Rights:

To request data deletion, simply send a /delete command to Vogadu via Messenger. Your request will be processed promptly.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We do not use cookies or other tracking technologies beyond those employed by the Messenger platform, which are used to analyze user engagement and usage rates.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We will comuumicate any changes to this Privacy Policy through our Messenger chat bot. We encourage you to periodically review this policy for any updates.
